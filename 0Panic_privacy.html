Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026 · Aloium

Short version: 0PANIC does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything happens on your device. We know nothing about you.
1. Who we are

0PANIC is developed and maintained by Aloium, based in Milan, Italy. This privacy policy applies to the 0PANIC iOS application and any associated websites, including this one.

2. Data we collect

None.

0PANIC does not require an account. It does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or health data.

The app runs entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers because we do not operate servers that receive user data.

3. Analytics and tracking

0PANIC does not include any analytics SDK, crash reporting service, or advertising framework. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or any tracking technology.

We do not know how many times you have used the app, when you used it, or what happened during a session.

4. TestFlight beta (beta users only)

If you are using 0PANIC through Apple TestFlight, Apple may collect certain diagnostic and usage data as part of their standard TestFlight service. This data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy, not ours. We do not have access to it.

If you choose to send us feedback directly by email, we will only use that information to improve the app and will not share it with third parties.

5. Health and sensitive information

0PANIC is a personal wellness tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice or diagnosis. The app does not access Apple Health, HealthKit, or any health-related APIs.

Any stress or anxiety-related information you might share with us voluntarily (e.g. in a feedback email) is kept strictly confidential and is never shared, sold, or disclosed to any third party.

6. Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs at runtime. The landing website uses Google Fonts, loaded from Google's CDN. Google's privacy policy applies to that request.

7. Children

0PANIC is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices — for example, by adding analytics to understand how the app is used — we will update this page and change the effective date above. We will never introduce data collection without being transparent about it.
